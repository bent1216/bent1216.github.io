About Project

1. Each group expected to develop any multimedia applications related with interactive books, website development, interactive flash/html games, Android/IOS applications/games and so on.

2. The application or project should consists FOUR (4) main elements in multimedia: images, audio, video and interactivity.

3. You can choose any authoring software to develop your application such as iBook Author (Mac OS), Adobe Flash, Eclipse or others.

4. Each group also required to provide ORIGINAL video related to the topic chosen. It must be at least TWO (2) videos with duration 2-4 minutes each. Marks will be given according to content, quality and creativity. The videos can be integrated to the application or run separately. Recommended software: iMovie (Mac OS/IOS).

5. The video must not violates rules and regulations of the University or any sensitive issues.
